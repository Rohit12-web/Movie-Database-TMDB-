Our Mission

TMDB is built for movie lovers, helping users explore, discover, and keep track of their favorite films and TV shows effortlessly.

Our History

TMDB was founded in 2024 with a passion for cinema. What started as a hobby project has now evolved into a fully functional platform for film enthusiasts worldwide.

How It Works

  1. Search for any movie or TV show using our advanced search tool.
  2. View detailed information, including cast, crew, and reviews.
  3. Discover trending, top-rated, and upcoming releases.
  4. Create and manage a personalized watchlist.
  5. Enjoy curated recommendations based on your interests.
