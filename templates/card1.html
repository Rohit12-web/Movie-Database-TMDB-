Reviews


Vijay Kumar


Jatt & Juliet 3 delivers a refreshing dose of Punjabi humor with Diljit Dosanjh and Neeru Bajwa reprising their iconic roles. The film masterfully blends comedy and romance, offering a mix of laugh-out-loud moments and heartfelt emotions. The chemistry between the leads is as strong as ever, and the supporting cast adds to the charm. While the plot is somewhat predictable, the engaging screenplay and energetic performances keep the audience hooked. A must-watch for those who love lighthearted entertainers!


Jashan Singh


Packed with hilarious sequences and engaging storytelling, Jatt & Juliet 3 successfully carries forward the legacy of the beloved franchise. The film thrives on its sharp dialogues, energetic performances, and vibrant Punjabi culture. Diljit Dosanjh shines with his impeccable comic timing, while Neeru Bajwa complements him perfectly with her charm. The music adds to the film’s appeal, making it an entertaining watch for all ages. Fans of the series won’t be disappointed!


Parul Kaur


Jatt & Juliet 3 is a delightful continuation of the fan-favorite Punjabi rom-com series. The movie stays true to its roots with a strong dose of comedy, romance, and misunderstandings that lead to hilarious situations. The cinematography beautifully captures the lively spirit of Punjab, and the foot-tapping music enhances the experience. While the narrative treads familiar ground, the sheer energy and chemistry between Diljit and Neeru keep the audience engaged till the very end. A fun, feel-good entertainer!
